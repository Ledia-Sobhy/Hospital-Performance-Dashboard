# 🏥 Hospital Performance & Operational Efficiency Dashboard

An Executive Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze **1,050+ patient records, 810 visits, and active physician performance** across multiple cities. This dashboard provides high-level financial tracking, clinical diagnostics, and resource allocation insights to optimize hospital management and enhance service recovery.

##  Executive Insights & Diagnostic Findings

Deep analytical drilling into the hospital's data revealed critical operational and financial trends:

1. **Financial Health & Revenue Drivers:** * Generated a total revenue of **12,031,458 L.E** within the monitored period.
   * The revenue split across payment methods is highly balanced: **Visa (35.78%)**, **Cash (32.67%)**, and **Medical Insurance (31.56%)**.
   * **Alexandria** emerged as the primary geographical hotspot generating **1,129,794 L.E**, closely followed by Tanta and Asyut.

2. **Clinical Specialization & Cost Efficiency:**
   * **General Surgery** (~1.33M L.E) and **Diabetes** (~1.29M L.E) are the highest revenue-generating diagnostic categories.
   * Conversely, **Orthopedics** and **Pneumonia** exhibit higher baseline treatment costs, signaling a critical need to optimize service delivery costs in these departments.

3. **Physician Performance & Operational Excellence:**
   * Monitored the productivity of **10 active physicians**.
   * **Dr. Iyad Amer** achieved the highest operational efficiency, emerging as the top revenue contributor with **748,353 L.E**.
   * Patient distribution remains highly balanced with no severe operational bottlenecks on any single doctor.
   * The hospital maintained an optimal Average Length of Stay (**LOS**) of **3.81 days** per patient, showcasing stable service quality.

##  Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated

* **Data Engineering & Architecture:** Utilized Power Query (Advanced Excel) to clean, profile, and transform raw healthcare data efficiently.
* **Advanced Analytics:** Formulated complex DAX measures in Power BI to calculate dynamic invoice averages (Average Patient Revenue: **11,459 L.E**), length of stay (LOS), and patient counts.
* **Visual Storytelling:** Designed a professional, high-impact user interface utilizing interactive slicers, cross-filtering, time-series revenue trends, and geo-mapping.
